Privacy Policy

SmartFillAI · Last updated: June 2026

Short version: SmartFillAI stores your profile data locally on your own device. Nothing is sold. Nothing is shared with advertisers. AI features send only field labels to external providers — never your personal values.

1. What We Collect

SmartFillAI collects only what you explicitly provide:

  • Profile data — name, contact info, work history, education, and other fields you add manually or import from a resume. Stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local and IndexedDB.
  • Remembered answers — questions and answers the extension learns from your form submissions. Stored locally in IndexedDB.
  • Uploaded documents — resume files and other documents you upload. Stored locally in IndexedDB; never transmitted unless you enable cloud sync.
  • Account email — if you create an account for cloud sync (optional). Stored in Supabase under your own private row-level-security-protected account.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or URLs you visit
  • Form values you type on websites (only the field labels are ever sent to AI)
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or analytics data
  • Any data from non-form page content

3. AI Features — What Is Sent to Third Parties

SmartFillAI uses AI to classify ambiguous form fields and generate essay-style answers. The following data is sent to GROQ or Google Gemini when those features are used:

  • Field classification (Step 6): Only field label text (e.g., "First Name", "Work Authorization"). Never your actual profile values.
  • Essay generation: The field label/question text and, if you have one, your stored essay preference (a seed text you control). Never your full profile.
  • Resume parsing: The resume text or PDF you upload, processed only to extract structured profile entries. The resume is sent once; SmartFillAI does not retain a copy on any server.

These requests are made from your browser's extension service worker directly to the AI provider. SmartFillAI does not have an intermediary server that receives or logs these requests.

4. Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you create a SmartFillAI account and enable cloud sync, your profile data is:

  • Transmitted over HTTPS (TLS) to Supabase, a third-party database provider.
  • Stored in a row-level-security (RLS) protected table accessible only to your user account.
  • Not used for any purpose other than letting you sync your profile across your own devices.
  • Deletable at any time: use Settings → Wipe All Data, which removes local data and triggers server-side deletion.

Cloud sync is off by default. Local-only mode works fully without an account.

5. Data Retention

  • All local data persists until you uninstall the extension or use Settings → Wipe All Data.
  • Cloud sync data is retained until you delete it via the wipe feature or contact us.
  • AI requests are subject to GROQ's and Google's own data retention policies. We do not send personally identifiable values to these services.

6. No Advertising or Data Sales

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third party for advertising purposes. There are no tracking pixels, no analytics SDKs, and no monetisation of user data.

7. Children

SmartFillAI is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and notify users via a popup notice in the extension on next open.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@smartfillai.com.
